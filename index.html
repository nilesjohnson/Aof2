Instructions

Clicking each dot shows a menu. Click "Adem" or "Milnor" to change the info displayed in the box. Note that it now takes a noticeable amount of time for mathjax to render everything on the page...if this becomes too annoying, we may have to deal with it.

Clicking away from a dot now clears the menu!

Click the different \(Sq^n\) to toggle display of that information. Currently the drawing is not updated immediately when you click the buttons, but that will appear soon.
